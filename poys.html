(P)AINT (O)N (Y)OUR (S)HIT

A visual archive of our works combining paint and cloth. This idea started with the faking of band merch to combat the high cost that is often attributed to such objects. The idea slowly devolved into our own ideas, every once and a while going back to our roots. Most of this work was quick and visceral, snap decisions and overly DIY methods are involved. Its goofy, incorrect, fun, and a fantastic metaphor for SHRED ALOHA.
